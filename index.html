New City College

New City College, as an online learning platform, is a leading provider of education and training in East London and Essex. Offering a diverse array of courses tailored for school leavers, adults, higher education seekers, apprentices, and individuals in need of foundation learning, we are dedicated to assisting learners in achieving their academic and career goals. Our online platform extends a warm welcome to students worldwide, fostering a rich and inclusive learning environment

Features

Age 16 to 18

We offer the widest range of courses for school leavers of any provider in East London and Essex. Get the skills you need to start a job, progress in a career, or go on to higher education at university.

Adult Courses

Retrain, upskill or do something you love. Many of our courses are FREE for adults!

Higher Education

Achieving your higher education qualification at New City College is a really convenient alternative to university. Our qualifications are designed to give you the skills that put your knowledge to practical use and are highly valued by employers both in the UK and overseas.

Apprenticeship

An apprenticeship is a real job, with a contract and holiday leave, where you learn, gain experience, and get paid.

Foundation Learning

NCC offer a comprehensive range of training and support to learners with Special Needs and Disabilities to help them achieve their aspirations.

International

New City College offers a range of courses for international students aged 16-18 and 19+. Please don't hesitate to contact our friendly International Team if you have any questions.
